## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Company-first research structure
The research case MUST begin from the company's own greatness before discussing institutional ownership or operation behavior.

#### Scenario: Visa research uses the required order
- **WHEN** the Visa research document is created
- **THEN** it MUST present company greatness, greatness formation, greatness evidence, and greatness identification before institutional buy/hold/sell analysis

### Requirement: Seven-part case template
The research case MUST use seven sections: why the company is great, how it became great, what proves greatness, how to identify similar greatness, institutional buy/hold/sell behavior, investor lessons and reflections, and current recommendation with archive category.

#### Scenario: Case template is complete
- **WHEN** a reader opens the Visa research document
- **THEN** the document MUST include all seven sections with company-first sections preceding the institutional behavior section

### Requirement: Evidence-based greatness judgement
The research case MUST distinguish between narrative claims, business model evidence, operating evidence, financial evidence, and institutional behavior evidence.

#### Scenario: Greatness evidence is explicit
- **WHEN** the document argues that Visa is a great company
- **THEN** it MUST cite concrete evidence such as business model structure, long-term development milestones, financial quality, or current operating data rather than relying only on institutional ownership

### Requirement: Valuation-aware current recommendation
The research case MUST include a current recommendation that combines business quality, current valuation state, structural risk, and archive category.

#### Scenario: Current recommendation is actionable for the research library
- **WHEN** the Visa research reaches its conclusion
- **THEN** it MUST classify Visa for the research library without framing the classification as a short-term buy or sell instruction

### Requirement: Skill creation after first case
The initial reusable skill MUST be created only after the Visa research case and archive entry exist.

#### Scenario: Skill follows proven workflow
- **WHEN** the skill is created
- **THEN** it MUST encode the workflow demonstrated by the Visa case and describe later company research as future iteration input
