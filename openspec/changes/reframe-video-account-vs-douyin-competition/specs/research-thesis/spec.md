## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Research thesis defines the primary question
The research package SHALL define one primary question for the Video Accounts vs Douyin study and SHALL break it into named sub-questions covering product purpose, strategic role, competitive boundary, ecosystem integration, monetization, and AI/search implications.

#### Scenario: Analyst opens the thesis frame
- **WHEN** the analyst starts the study from the research package
- **THEN** the package presents one primary question and a stable set of sub-questions

### Requirement: Research thesis declares falsifiable hypotheses
The research package SHALL state at least three falsifiable hypotheses and SHALL identify the evidence types that can strengthen or weaken each hypothesis.

#### Scenario: Analyst evaluates a thesis claim
- **WHEN** the analyst reviews a major claim about Video Accounts or Douyin
- **THEN** the package shows the claim as a hypothesis together with supporting and disconfirming evidence criteria

### Requirement: Research thesis tracks confidence updates
The research package SHALL support updating the confidence level of each hypothesis as new evidence is added.

#### Scenario: New evidence is added to the archive
- **WHEN** a new source materially supports or challenges a hypothesis
- **THEN** the package records a corresponding confidence update for that hypothesis
