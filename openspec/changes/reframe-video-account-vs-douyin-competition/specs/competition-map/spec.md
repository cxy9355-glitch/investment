## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Competition map compares the platforms as systems
The research output SHALL compare Video Accounts and Douyin across strategy, distribution mechanism, creator supply, monetization and transactions, ecosystem integration, and AI/search extension.

#### Scenario: Analyst reads the comparison output
- **WHEN** the analyst opens the competition map
- **THEN** both platforms are compared across all six dimensions in a consistent format

### Requirement: Competition map explains mechanisms, not just metrics
The research output SHALL explain how each platform acquires attention, converts intent, and closes monetization loops rather than listing performance metrics alone.

#### Scenario: Analyst reviews a comparison section
- **WHEN** the analyst reads a dimension such as monetization or distribution
- **THEN** the section includes the underlying mechanism as well as key evidence or metrics

### Requirement: Competition map ends with thesis implications
The research output SHALL conclude with implications for Tencent's strategic position, the strongest counterarguments, and the highest-priority open questions.

#### Scenario: Analyst finishes the map
- **WHEN** the analyst reaches the end of the competition map
- **THEN** the output summarizes what the comparison means, what could falsify the thesis, and what should be researched next
