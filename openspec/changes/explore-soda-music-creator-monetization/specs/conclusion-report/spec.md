## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Conclusion report separates monetization layers
The final report SHALL distinguish direct platform income, platform support with monetary implications, and downstream monetization spillover.

#### Scenario: Analyst reads the conclusion
- **WHEN** the analyst reviews the final memo
- **THEN** the memo separates what Soda Music pays directly from what exposure may enable indirectly

### Requirement: Conclusion report states confidence and limitations
The final report SHALL state confidence, evidence limits, and open questions where payout transparency is incomplete.

#### Scenario: Analyst wants to use the report for decision-making
- **WHEN** the analyst relies on the memo
- **THEN** the memo makes clear which conclusions are strong, which are tentative, and what remains unknown
