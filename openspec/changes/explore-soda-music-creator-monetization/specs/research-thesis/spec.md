## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Research thesis defines the practical income question
The research package SHALL define one primary question for Soda Music creator monetization and SHALL break it into stable sub-questions covering creator entry, direct revenue, incentive mechanisms, downstream monetization, and evidence limits.

#### Scenario: Analyst starts the exploration
- **WHEN** the analyst opens the research package
- **THEN** the package presents one primary question and a fixed set of sub-questions tied to creator economics

### Requirement: Research thesis states falsifiable hypotheses
The research package SHALL state at least three falsifiable hypotheses and SHALL identify the evidence that can strengthen or weaken each one.

#### Scenario: Analyst evaluates a major claim
- **WHEN** the analyst reviews a conclusion about creator monetization
- **THEN** the package shows the conclusion as a hypothesis with explicit supporting and disconfirming evidence criteria
